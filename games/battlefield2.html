About the Game

Battlefield 2 brings the intensity and chaos of Battlefield 1942 into the modern era, featuring new weapons and vehicles, along with one of the most influential squad systems in the history of first-person shooters.

The game features a next-generation engine (for its time), with a new physics system and dynamic lighting to make the battlefield more realistic.

Here at The OG Servers, we focus on preserving the pure BF2 experience, ensuring that Commander mechanics and squad cooperation remain the heart of every match.
